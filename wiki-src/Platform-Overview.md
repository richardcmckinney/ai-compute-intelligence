# Platform Overview

## Problem
Enterprise AI spending is growing faster than governance controls. Teams can often answer "how much did we spend?" but not reliably answer:
- Which team caused the spend?
- Which model/provider choices were avoidable?
- Which interventions reduce cost without quality regression?

## Product Thesis
The platform wins at the decision moment, not only after-the-fact reporting.

## Three Layers
1. Measure: ingest usage and cost telemetry.
2. Attribute: resolve ownership via graph + heuristic reconciliation.
3. Intervene: deliver policy and optimization signals with fail-open safety.

## Deployment Modes
- Passive: observe and report.
- Advisory: enrich requests/responses with guidance.
- Active: route/redirect only when equivalence and policy conditions pass.

## Why This Is Defensible
- Cross-system reconciliation depth.
- Confidence calibration and governance.
- Operationally safe intervention architecture.
- Integration-driven workflow lock-in.
